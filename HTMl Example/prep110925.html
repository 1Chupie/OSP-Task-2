Why Combustion engines are better than EV's

"The best or nothing" -Das Beste oder Nichts

Point 1

Combustion engines are more efficient for long-distance travel due to their higher energy density compared to current battery technology.As a result, they can cover greater distances without the need for frequent refueling or recharging stops.This makes them more practical for road trips and long commutes, where access to charging infrastructure may be limited.In conclusion, combustion engines offer superior range and convenience for long-distance travel compared to electric vehicles.

Point 2

Combustion engines provide more feedback to the driver and a more engaging driving experience. The sound, vibration, and responsiveness of a combustion engine can enhance the connection between the driver and the vehicle, making driving more enjoyable for enthusiasts. Additionally, the ability to modulate power delivery through throttle control allows for a more dynamic driving experience. In contrast, electric vehicles often have a quieter and more subdued driving experience, which may not appeal to all drivers. Therefore, combustion engines can offer a more thrilling and interactive driving experience compared to electric vehicles.

Conclusion

  • Longer Range
  • More Engaging Driving Experience
  • Familiarity and Infrastructure
  • Lower Initial Cost
  • Faster Refueling
  • Variety of Models
  • Performance Options
